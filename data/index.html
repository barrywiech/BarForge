Pump Calibration Wizard

Calibration runs each pump for 5 seconds. Catch the output in a measuring cup and enter the actual volume.

1 Place a measuring cup under the nozzle for the selected pump.
2 Click Start Run — pump will run for 5 seconds.
3 Enter the measured volume and save.
